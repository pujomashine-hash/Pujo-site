     NJOO KWETU ROHO MWEMA 

1.Njoo kwetu roho mwema 
  mfariji wetu,
  tufundishe ya mbinguni,
  Tuwe watu wapya.

//Njoo njoo njoo roho mwema X2

2.Kwa huruma tubariki 
  tuishi na wewe,
  Tukiwaza na kunena
  Tuongozwe nawe.

//Njoo njoo njoo roho........

3.Roho mwema mfariji,
  utupe hekima,
  tukiwaza na kunena,
  Yote yawe yako.

//Njoo njoo...................

4.Tudumishe tuwe hai ,
  Na ukweli wako,
  tukaishi kikundini,
  Kanisani mwako.

//Njoo njoo njoo roho mwema X2 


English:Gracious spirit ✅
Sources: T.M 44,TMW 136,PUJO HYMNS 13.
Mtunzi: wilson niwagila

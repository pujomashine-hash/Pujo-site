   JESUS LOVER OF MY SOUL 


1.Jesus, lover of my soul,
  Jesus, I will trust in Thee;
  All my fears I lay before Thee,
  All my hopes and all my fears,
  All my cares and all my tears,
  Jesus, I will trust in Thee.

2.Hide me, O my Savior, hide,
  Till the storm of life is past;
  O receive my soul at last,
  In the secret of Thy side.
  As the shades of life are falling,
  Let Thy presence be my light.

3.Other refuge have I none,
  Hangs my helpless soul on Thee;
  Leave, O leave me not alone,
  Still my heart to calm and cheer,
  O let Thy word create in me
  A strong and steadfast hope to bear.

4.Thou, O Christ, art all I want;
  More than all in Thee I find;
  Raise the fallen, cheer the faint,
  Heal the sick, and lead the blind.
  In Thy promise I delight,
  There my trust, my heart, my all I lay.

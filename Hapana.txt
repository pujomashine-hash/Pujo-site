          HAPANA RAFIKI KAMA YESU 


1.Hapana Rafiki Kama yesu 
 yu.. peke yu.. peke
 Hapana mpozi kama yesu 
 yu peke yu peke

//yesu ajua shida zetu 
  Aweza kutuongoza 
  Hapana Rafiki Kama yesu 
  yu pekeyu peke,

2.Yeye anaye sifa ni yesu
  yu peke yu peke,
  Ndiye aliye mnyenyekevu 
  yu peke yu peke 

//yesu ajua shida zetu........

3.Yesu pamoja nasi daima
  yu peke yu peke
  kwa usiku aleta salama,
  yu peke yu peke,

 //yesu ajua shida zetu......

4.kulinda yesu yu mwaminifu 
  yu peke yu peke 
  Atawakubali wahalifu,
  yu peke yu peke 

//yesu ajua shida zetu...........

5.Alipelekwa awe mwokozi 
  yu peke yu peke
  Anatutayarishia enzi 
  yu peke yu peke,

//yesu ajua shida zetu...........


English: There is not a friend like a lowly Jesus {Not not one}✅
Sources:  Tenzi 155,Nyimbo za injli 113,T.R 3,yn 15:14                 ,               TMW 398
Mtunga maneno: Charles Wesley 1707-1788
Mtunga sauti: T.jarman

1.O thou joyful day,
  O thou blessed day,
  gladsome, peaceful Christmastide.
  Earth’s hope awaken,
  Love life has taken.
  Joy, O, joy to all at Christmastide.

2.O sanctissima,
  O sanctissima,
  gladsome, peaceful Christmastide.
  Light now is beaming,
  our souls redeeming.
  Joy, O, joy to all at Christmastide.

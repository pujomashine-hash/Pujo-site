 LIKO LANGO MOJA WAZI 

 
1. Liko lango moja wazi,
   Ni lango la mbinguni;
   Na wote waingiao
   Watapata nafasi. 
 
//Lango ndiye Yesu Bwana
  Wote waingie kwake,
  Lango! Lango
  La Mbinguni ni wazi.
 
2. Yesu ndiye lango hili,
  Hata sasa ni wazi,
  Kwa wakubwa na wadogo,
  Tajiri na maskini.
//Lango ndiye..........
 
3. Hili ni lango la raha,
   Ni lango la rehema;
   Kila mtu apitaye
   Hana majonzi tena.
//Lango ndiye........
 
4. Tukipita lango hili
   Tutatua mizigo,
   Tuliochukua kwanza,
   Tutavikwa uzima.
//Lango ndiye.......
 
5. Hima ndugu tuingie
   Lango halijafungwa,
   Likifungwa mara moja
   Halitafunguliwa.
//Lango ndiye..........
 
Sources: Tenzi121.
English: The gate ajar for me.

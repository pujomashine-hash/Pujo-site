  KUMTEGEMEA MWOKOZI 

 
1. Kumtegemea Mwokozi,
   Kwangu tamu kabisa;
   Kukubali neno lake
   Nina raha moyoni.
 
//Yesu,Yesu namwamini,
  Nimemwona thabiti;
  Yesu,Yesu,yu thamani,
  Ahadi zake kweli. 
 
2.Kumtegemea Mwokozi,
  Kwangu tamu kabisa,
  Kuamini damu yake
  Nimeoshwa kamili.
//Yesu  Yesu namwamini....
 
3.Kumtegemea Mwokozi,
  Kwangu tamu kabisa,
  Kwake daima napata
  Uzima na amani.
//Yesu Yesu namwamini.......
 
4.Nafurahi kwa sababu
  Nimekutegemea;
  Yesu,Mpendwa na Rafiki
  Uwe nami dawamu. 
//Yesu Yesu namwamini.........
 

Sources: PUJO HYMNS 38, Tenzi 16.
English Hymn:Tis so sweet to trust in Jesus ✅

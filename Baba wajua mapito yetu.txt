  BABA WAJUA MAPITO YETU

1.Baba wajua mapito yetu 
  Maisha yetu, na matendo yetu,
  Siri ifichikayo kwa mwanadamu,
  kwako Baba iwazi.

//Tusaide Yesu, tusaidie Yesu,
  tusaide mwokozi, tusipotee,
  Tusaide Yesu, tusaidie Yesu 
  tusaide mwokozi, tusiangamie.

2.Ndugu unayesikia neno,
  Lake Mungu leo,
  Chagua, utakayemtumikia,
  kama ni Mungu au shetani.

//Rekebisha leo, mwenendo wako ndugu,
  Ili usipotee,
  Rekebisha leo, mwenendo wako ndugu,
  Ili usihangamie.

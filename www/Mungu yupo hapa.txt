   𝑴𝑼𝑵𝑮𝑼 𝒀𝑼𝑷𝑶 𝑯𝑨𝑷𝑨 

1.Mungu yupo hapa 
  Na tumsujudie
  tumche tuka mtumikie
  yu pamoja nasi ,
  nyamanzeni wote,
  tumlalamikie mponya ,
  wanao litaja 
  jina lake njooni 
  tumpeni sadaka.

2.Mungu yupo hapa ,
  anayesifiwa,
  na malaika kila siku,
  wote wanaimba ,
  Mungu mtakatifu,
  aheshimiwe popote,
  ee Mungu sikia 
  tukikuimbia,
  Na sisi wadogo.

3.Twataka kuacha,
  mambo ya dunia,
  na uzuri wake wote,
  mioyo na mapenzi 
  hata nia zetu,
  twakutolea Bwana 
  wewe tu u Mungu ,
  Anayestahili 
  sifa za viumbe.

4.Mwokozi ingia
  mioyo yetu yote,
  Iwe nyumba yako wewe,
  ndiwe Bwana wetu,
  utusaidie 
  tukupende siku zote,
  tukiwa wako tu,
  tukae na Wewe 
  Tukusujidie.

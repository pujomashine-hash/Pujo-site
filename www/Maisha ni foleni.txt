Maisha ni foleni,zamu yako 
inakuja usimwache Mungu sababu,
umekosa wewe pambana, zamu yako 
ipo zamu yako ikifika shida uta
zisahau Mungu yu upande wako 
atakufuta machozi.

mateso yakizidi kaza usichoke 
ujue ukombozi wako umefika.  X2

This life,life is good your turn 
is gone don't leave God because 
you have nothing you keep fighting 
your turn is come I'll forget the 
trouble God is on your side he'll 
wipe your tears your sufferings

if your sufferings get out, no
where that your redemption has come. X2
